Model Evaluation Metrics

Understanding the Performance and Accuracy of the Prediction Model

View Dashboard

Accuracy

Measures the overall correctness of the model’s predictions.

Precision

Indicates how many predicted risk cases were actually correct.

Recall

Shows how effectively the model identifies students at risk.

F1 Score

Harmonic mean of precision and recall for balanced evaluation.

Why Model Evaluation Matters

Evaluating the performance of the prediction model ensures that the system accurately identifies students at risk of digital addiction. Reliable evaluation metrics help validate the effectiveness of the machine learning algorithms used in the Digital Addiction Risk Assessment System.

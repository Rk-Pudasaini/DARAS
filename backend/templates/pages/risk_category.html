Digital Addiction Risk Categories

Understanding Different Levels of Digital Addiction Risk Among Students

Run Risk Assessment

Low Risk

Students show healthy digital usage patterns. Their screen time and online behaviors remain balanced with academic and social activities.

Moderate Risk

Students demonstrate signs of increased screen dependency, including longer online sessions and reduced offline activities.

High Risk

Students exhibit strong digital addiction symptoms such as excessive screen time, sleep disruption, and reduced academic focus.

How Risk Levels Are Determined

Risk categories are calculated using data-driven machine learning models based on students' digital behavior patterns, screen time, sleep quality, and psychological indicators. The system helps educators and policymakers identify students who may require early intervention.
